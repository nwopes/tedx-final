About TEDxSimplexity

In a world of increasing complexity, the most powerful solutions often emerge from simple ideas. TEDxSimplexity explores this duality through inspiring talks that reveal the elegance within complexity.

Our 2023 conference brings together thinkers, creators, and innovators who demonstrate how simplicity and complexity can coexist to create meaningful change.

November 15, 2023
9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
